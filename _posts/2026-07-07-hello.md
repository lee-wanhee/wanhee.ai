Welcome. This is the first post on my new site at [wanhee.ai](https://wanhee.ai).

I do research on **3D scene understanding** and **world models**, and I've wanted a place to
write about it that isn't a paper — somewhere to think out loud, work through ideas, and share
things that don't fit neatly into a publication.

Here's roughly what I expect to write about:

- **Research notes** — problems I'm working on, and the reasoning behind design choices.
- **Paper walkthroughs** — explaining work I find interesting (mine and others').
- **Practical bits** — code, experiments, and things I wish someone had written down for me.

The blog supports the usual things I'll need. Inline math like $$e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$, display math:

$$
\mathcal{L}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{x \sim \mathcal{D}} \left[ \| f_\theta(x) - y \|^2 \right]
$$

and code blocks with highlighting:

```python
def render(scene, camera):
    """A world model turns a scene + viewpoint into an observation."""
    return scene.project(camera)
```
