Images and video are continuous, and next-token prediction does not transfer cleanly. Diffusion
models instead learn to reverse a gradual noising process, turning generation into a sequence of
denoising steps. This chapter covers the continuous-data toolkit and what it shares with — and
borrows from — the discrete case.
