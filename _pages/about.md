I'm Wanhee Lee. I work on **3D scene understanding** and **world models** — teaching machines to
perceive, represent, and reason about the physical 3D world from visual observation.

This site is where I write about research I'm doing and thinking through — a mix of technical notes,
paper walkthroughs, and half-formed ideas. New posts show up on the [blog](/blog/), and my code
lives on [GitHub](https://github.com/lee-wanhee).
