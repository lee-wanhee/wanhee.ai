I'm a PhD candidate at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu), advised by
[Dan Yamins](https://stanford.edu/~yamins/). My research focuses on **scalable autoregressive
transformers** for visual perception, controllable generation, and multimodal reasoning over
spatiotemporal data. I develop unified models that integrate RGB, depth, optical flow, and camera
motion — enabling tasks like novel view synthesis, object manipulation, and geometry extraction.

I also write about research and ideas on the [blog](/blog/). You can reach me by email (icon below),
on [Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/lee_wanhee_), or find my code on [GitHub](https://github.com/lee-wanhee).
